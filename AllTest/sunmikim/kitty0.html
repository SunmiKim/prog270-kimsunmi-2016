Pixie cares

Pixie was sent to an animal shelter when she was two months old where she got spayed and had immunization shots. She may not have noticed the fact that she had to leave her mother after she become two months old. In the shelter, she was with other peer friends right next to her square cubicle. She was very active and liked to play with almost every objects including papers, balls, and strings. The only problem was that she couldn't run in that small cubicle. However, that is no longer a problem as she's found a new home after spending in the shelter for just a week or two. In the new home, she has many new friends and she is now caring for other animal friends.
